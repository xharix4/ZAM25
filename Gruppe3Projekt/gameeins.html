Fish Fishing Humans

What a fintastic day!

It's high time for some good, old, humane fishing. These cute pets won't catch themselves after all, let's go to the surface and jump on their backs! I'll drag them to the undercity, the Pet Traders are just waiting to fill my pockets with shiny shells. I'll have to make sure my fragile humans get enough air, my bubble magic ought to do the trick!

Controls:

You'll want to jump on the humans to begin dragging them to the Undercity, once that happens a bubble will appear engulfing you. The bubble will flash randomly - that's when you'll want to press spacebar! Once down in Fish City you'll get your deserved Fish Dollars.

WASD: Move
Spacebar: Jump & Sustain Bubble
